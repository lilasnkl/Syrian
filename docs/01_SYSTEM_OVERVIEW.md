# 01 System Overview

## Purpose
Syrian Services is a marketplace platform connecting customers with service providers through a managed, role-aware workflow.

## Users
- Customer: posts requests, evaluates bids, tracks progress, reviews providers.
- Provider: manages profile/services, bids on requests, executes jobs, receives reviews.
- Admin: moderates users/providers/content and monitors marketplace health.
- Moderator: handles disputes and moderation workflows under admin policy.

## Core Business Capabilities
- Authentication and authorization
- Provider discovery and profile management
- Service listing management
- Order/request lifecycle management
- Bidding lifecycle management
- Messaging and notifications
- Reviews and disputes/complaints
- Admin and moderation operations

## Non-Functional Goals
- Maintainable modular architecture
- Predictable API contracts
- Strong permission and workflow controls
- Incremental frontend modernization without destructive rewrites
- Testability across domain boundaries
